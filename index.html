Sports Analysis

The mettic used in order to rank these players is, Goals Scored, Assists, Games Played, Goals per game, Shots on Target, Big Chances Created, Dribbles, Goals with head, Shooting Accuracy, Pass Completion Percentage. The formula used was pretty simple as we just added up Goals Scored, Assists, and Games Played and the divided by 3. Then we added up the rest of the statistical categories and divided by 7. Finally, we added the two numbers in order to get their rating.
